    Our Mission


    Youth Con is a non-profit organization that helps
    youth cultivate into leaders in their community through Christian ministry. Each year, we organize an event called Youth Con hosted by multiple churches in Southern California.
    This Event will be held in Indonesian Presbyterian Church in Fullerton on June 1st, 2019.

    The purpose of YouthCon is to connect and form a community of young believers to grow and learn about Jesus Christ together. It is a full day conference where the youth will be able to explore, share, and connect with their peers and encourage one another in their walk with the Lord.

    This is our third year and we have seen wonderful results. Each year we have grown in numbers. Our first year, we had 3 churches in attendance with 40 members. Our second year, we more than doubled. This year, we would like to triple our attendance. ‍
